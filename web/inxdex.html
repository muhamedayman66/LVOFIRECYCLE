About IRECYCLE

At IRECYCLE, we are driven by a passion for creating a sustainable future through innovative recycling solutions. Our core mission is to empower individuals and communities to significantly reduce waste, actively promote eco-friendly practices, and make a tangible contribution to a healthier planet.

By harnessing the power of technology, we strive to make recycling more accessible, highly efficient, and genuinely rewarding for everyone involved. We firmly believe that every small action collectively makes a big difference. Join us on our exciting journey to transform waste into valuable opportunities and collaboratively build a greener tomorrow, one conscious step at a time.

AI-Powered Recycling Classification

Our AI-powered system leverages advanced deep learning to streamline the recycling process. Using a fine-tuned MobileNetV2 model, IRECYCLE accurately identifies recyclable materials, achieving a test accuracy of 91.66%. Here's how our AI enhances recycling efficiency:

Accurate Material Classification

Our AI model, trained on a dataset of 12,594 images, identifies Aluminum Cans, Glass Bottles, and Plastic Bottles with high precision, helping users sort recyclables correctly.

Real-Time Image Processing

Upload an image through the IRECYCLE app, and our FastAPI backend processes it instantly, delivering classification results with confidence scores to guide recycling decisions.

Robust and Scalable AI

Built with TensorFlow and optimized with techniques like dropout and L2 regularization, our model ensures robust performance, minimizing errors and supporting scalable recycling solutions.

With a validation accuracy peaking at 94.15% during training, our AI system empowers users to recycle smarter, contributing to a cleaner and more sustainable environment.
